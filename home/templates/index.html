About

We at DOW are passionate about cars and models. And when we say passionate… we mean it. The driving force behind Dow is B.W. Smith. A true petrolhead. During his studies he started participating in historic rallies with an old Lancia Fulvia in 1998. Nowadays he owns an Alfa Romeo Giulia and a MGB. True timeless beauties form different eras and generations of pure automobile greatness.

In 2012 the Dow’s Model Car Workshop opened its virtual doors to offer its services. Today our customers of the commissioned customized model cars are located all around the globe. It is a truly international market we proudly serve. Dow are B.W. Smith, J.G.Wold and A. Roberts Sharing our love for anything motor related brought us together at University. Our different areas of expertise made us realise that a business venture together would set us apart from what was already out there. We bring you the highest of quality products and deep knowledge in the field, together with a a forward thinking attitude to manage the best deals for the most sought out items in the market.

At DOW we are enthusiasts as much as retailers. We don’t just want to sell you a model, we want you to love what you purchase! When you purchase a model from DOW you know that you are dealing with people who share your passion and take a pride in providing impeccable customer service.

We specialise in 1/43 and 1/18 scale model cars by manufacturers including C.A.R.S, Modelthat, Nr.one, Wheels, Precious and many others. As enthusiasts, our interests are mainly in European classics with French cars being particularly prominent. This enthusiasm stems from owning a long line of French cars and having a keen interest in all things automotive. But surely, we do not discriminate, and bring interesting additions to our collection from all corners of the car world. What matters to us is the quality. The models we stock are those which we like and offer good value. We are continually expanding our product range as we believe that it is important to not only offer great service and value for money, but also to offer the widest possible choice to our customers. The models we stock are those which we like and offer good value with affordable pricing.

If you do not find what you are looking for, stay tuned. There are many well-loved models in the pipeline which we will add to our catalog as they are released. Meanwhile, get to know our highlights contenders for this month. And don't forget our blog section for stories, intrigue and tidbits from our expert A. Roberts and other contributors.
